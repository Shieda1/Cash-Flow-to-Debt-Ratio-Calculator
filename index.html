What is the cash flow to debt ratio calculator?
The cash flow to debt ratio calculator is a tool that determines how much a company is indebted relative to the cash flow generated from operations during each fiscal period.
How to calculate cash flow to debt ratio?
To calculate cash flow to debt ratio you should use the following formula:
cash flow to debt ratio = (operating cash flow / total debt) * 100
